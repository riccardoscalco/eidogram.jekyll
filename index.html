# Science, Technology and Innovation in Italy

The map shows the role, in terms of innovation, played by the italian administrative regions. The statistics is presented by means of a cartogram, where the area of every region is proportional to the corresponding innovation effort.

data source: istat.it.
tools: topojson, d3-cartogram, D3js and Inkscape.

# Rohmetric: investigate Influenza virus evolution with entropic metric measures

Rohmetric is an initiative whose mission is to investigate and show the evolution of Influenza Virus by means of cutting edge mathematical methods. Different virus strains are identifying by horizontal colored lines, each point identifies the hemagglutinin segment of an isolate, isolates are full lenght and come from all over the world.

data source: GISAID.
tools: Python, Numpy, D3js, and Google charts.

# Positive and negative emotions on Twitter

The italian words 'amo', odio', 'felice', 'triste' ('love', 'hate', 'happy', 'sad') and others have been tracked on the public Twitter streaming during an ordinary Friday. Not a news, but still it's amazing how people freely express the most private feelings on social media.

tools: D3js, Twython and Dataset.

# Pykov: finite Markov chains with Python

Pykov is versatile python module useful to manipulate Markov chains. It is able to calculate various kind of quantities, like steady state distribution, mean first passage times, random walks, absorbing times, and so on.

tools: python.
