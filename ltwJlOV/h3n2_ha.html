Evolution of the hemagglutinin segment of H3N2 and H1N1 Influenza Virus.

The plot shows the temporal evolution of influenza virus H3N2 in the last years. Different virus strains are identifying by horizontal colored lines (each point identifies the hemagglutinin segment of an isolate, isolates are full lenght and come from all over the world). Note that virus strains do not appear suddenly, early signals are present even one year before in some cases.

According to the analysis, a new strain emerged on the winter season 2012/13 (clusters 40-44), the precursors of which go back to January 2012.
